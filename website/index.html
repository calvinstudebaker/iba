About PARQ

PARQ is a free iOS app designed to eliminate the stresses of parking in San Francisco. Focusing on safety, pricing, and avoiding tickets, Parq is the first application of its kind to show you the best spots that fit your specific parking needs.

Parq also helps you relax when you're no longer behind the wheel. Parq sends you real-time push notifications when your car is illegally parked or seems to be moving without you.

Parq relies upon publicly-available city data as well as user-generated reports to provide the most accurate, helpful, and up-to-date information about the spots around you. With Parq in hand, you can park fearlessly.
